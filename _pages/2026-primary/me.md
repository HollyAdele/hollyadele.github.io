I am running for Precinct Captain in Kettering 4K. I'm doing it because I believe in honest, curious conversations with people and coming to fair compromises. I believe in them, but I don't see them happening in national politics. So here I am, starting them in my own neighborhood. 

I support the Constitution as a safeguard against tyranny, truth through a diversity of viewpoints, peace through understanding, and prosperity through education. 

I also am affiliated with Team Kettering, a pro-democracy group that I'd love to get you in contact with. I have attended a candidate forum hosted by the South Dayton Democrats, and have spent hours researching and writing.
