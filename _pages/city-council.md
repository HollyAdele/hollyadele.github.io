I am not pleased with the Republican Party right now for these reasons: 
- They have voted four times to allow Trump to exercise the Senate's exclusive power to wage war. 
- They are removing “violent criminals” (immigrants) from their communities (most of the immigrants have no violent convictions) who “broke the law.” But the Republicans are letting Trump stay in the highest office regardless of his 34 convicted felonies.

These disasters affect the poor more than the rich, who are insulated by their money. I am concerned for the people who are suffering and will suffer. I am also concerned because unequal societies are unstable. (I like stable societies.)      

I like many Republican policies, or what Republican policies used to be. I like it when they encourage business and industry, prevent regulation from stifling growth, and protect the country and justice everywhere. I like when they are responsible with the country’s resources and encourage work and self-sufficiency. 

What I see right now is self-interested billionaires teaming up with white supremacists to exploit the dissatisfaction of men, rural voters, and religious people. Together, this group is dismantling the democratic society that the founding fathers gifted us. I think we need to listen to the true concerns of all these groups and find compromises so that no one wants to burn down the country anymore. Even if Trump is removed, we will still have all of his supporters who will feel the exact same way they did before. They will just find a new figurehead. 

I believe we need reconciliation, not retribution. We need to avoid the oscillation of violence that occurred during the French Revolution, the Rwandan civil war, and the English violence between Catholics and Protestants. We do this by listening and compromising to build a society where all can prosper. I don't see Republicans doing this right now, so I don't want to vote more into office.
